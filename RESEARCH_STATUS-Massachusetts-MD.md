## KEY INSIGHTS

1. **Well-Documented Program**
   - Massachusetts has comprehensive, clearly written CME requirements
   - Board policies are well-organized and accessible
   - Statutory authority is clear and specific

2. **Attestation-Based Verification**
   - No evidence of certificate upload requirement
   - Board relies on physician self-reporting
   - For-cause audits only (not routine)

3. **Topic Complexity**
   - 9 distinct CME requirements
   - Mix of one-time and recurring
   - Mix of mandatory and conditional
   - Allows Risk Management credits to offset certain topics

4. **License Type Variations**
   - Different hour requirements by license type
   - Different exemptions by license type
   - Administrative and Temporary Faculty licenses have reduced requirements

5. **Gaps Are Manageable**
   - No critical blocking issues
   - All gaps can be resolved with 2-3 phone calls + 2 document reviews
   - MVP is ready; enhancements are optional
