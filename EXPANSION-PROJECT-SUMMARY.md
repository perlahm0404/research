## ‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL REMINDERS

### Research Integrity
- ‚úÖ NEVER fabricate statute citations
- ‚úÖ NEVER guess at requirements
- ‚úÖ Document 3+ search attempts for gaps
- ‚úÖ Mark uncertainty with [INFERENCE] and confidence level
- ‚úÖ Cite sources with URLs and access dates

### Evidence Classification
- **[FACT - STATUTE]:** Direct from state law (quote + cite + URL)
- **[FACT - ADMIN_CODE]:** From administrative regulations (quote + cite + URL)
- **[FACT - BOARD]:** From official board website (URL + date)
- **[INFERENCE - CONFIDENCE]:** Derived from facts (reasoning + confidence)
- **[CRITICAL GAP]:** Not found after extensive search (3+ attempts + impact)

### Completion Percentage Calibration
- **50%:** Stub (150 lines, 15 citations, 7 sections)
- **70%:** Standard (300 lines, 25 citations, 10 sections)
- **85%:** Comprehensive (450 lines, 50 citations, 13-15 sections) ‚Üê TARGET
- **95%:** Reference-quality (600 lines, 75 citations, 15 sections)
