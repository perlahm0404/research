## Initial License Requirements

### Examination Requirements

**Accepted Examinations:**
- USMLE (United States Medical Licensing Examination)
- - NBME (National Board of Medical Examiners)
  - - FLEX (Federal Licensing Examination)
    - - LMCC (Licentiate of the Medical Council of Canada)
     
      - **Passing Score Standards:**
      - - Minimum: 75% OR the 3-digit USMLE minimum passing score as set by the USMLE program
        - - All steps of the licensure examination must be passed within 10 years
         
          - **Exam Combinations Allowed:**
          - Oklahoma allows specific combinations of examinations:
          - 1. NBME Part I or USMLE Step 1, PLUS NBME Part II or USMLE Step 2, PLUS NBME Part III or USMLE Step 3
            2. 2. FLEX component 1 PLUS USMLE Step 3
               3. 3. NBME Part I or USMLE Step 1, PLUS NBME Part II or USMLE Step 2, PLUS FLEX component 2
                 
                  4. **Exam Failure Limits:**
                  5. - Applicants failing any part of a licensing examination THREE times are not eligible for a license
                     - - Incomplete scores count as failures
                       - - If a combination of NBME, FLEX, and/or USMLE is utilized, applicants failing more than SIX total examinations are not eligible
                         - - Exception: Applicants with ABMS (American Board of Medical Specialties) board certification may request exception from the Board
                          
                           - ### Educational Requirements
                          
                           - **Medical Degree:**
                           - - US-trained applicants: Degree from LCME-accredited medical school
                             - - Foreign-trained applicants: Degree from ECFMG-certified medical school; must pass ECFMG examination and obtain ECFMG certificate
                              
                               - **Postgraduate Training:**
                               - - Required for most licensure pathways: 2+ years of postgraduate medical training
                                 - - Verified through:
                                   -   - Form 2: Verification of Completed Post-Graduate Training (Board form)
                                       -   - Form 4: Verification of Clinical Clerkship (Board form)
                                           -   - Form 1: Graduation Verification (Board form)
                                            
                                               - ### Background Check & Citizenship Requirements
                                            
                                               - **Background Check:**
                                               - - Extended Background Check (EBC) required in compliance with Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA)
                                                 - - Applicants must sign EBC consent form
                                                  
                                                   - **Citizenship/Immigration Status:**
                                                   - - US Citizens: Standard eligibility
                                                     - - Qualified Aliens: Eligible if presenting valid documentary evidence of one of the following:
                                                       -   1. Valid, unexpired immigrant or nonimmigrant visa status
                                                           2.   2. Pending or approved asylum application
                                                                3.   3. Refugee admission to US
                                                                     4.   4. Pending or approved temporary protected status (TPS) application
                                                                          5.   5. Approved deferred action status
                                                                               6.   6. Pending adjustment of status or conditional resident status
                                                                                 
                                                                                    7. **License Validity for Non-Citizens:**
                                                                                    8. - License card valid for the period of authorized stay in US
                                                                                       - - If no end date specified, license valid for one year only
                                                                                         - - Verification through SAVE Program (Systematic Alien Verification for Entitlements)
                                                                                          
                                                                                           - **Required Evidence:**
                                                                                           - - Evidence of Status Form (mandatory for licensure by endorsement or examination, or for reinstatement)
                                                                                             - - Must be submitted with application and supporting documentation
                                                                                              
                                                                                               - ### Application Processing
                                                                                              
                                                                                               - **Processing Timeline:** 8-12 weeks typical (may vary)
                                                                                              
                                                                                               - **Initial License Fee:** Varies by license type
                                                                                              
                                                                                               - **Application Method:**
                                                                                               - - Online application system available
                                                                                                 - - MD Initial License Application portal at: https://www.ok.gov/medlic/licensing/app/menu.php
                                                                                                   - - Note: "This link is for Medical Doctors (MD) Only. If you are applying for a DO license, please apply through the DO Board website"
                                                                                                    
                                                                                                     - **Licensure by Endorsement Alternative:**
                                                                                                     - - Applicants with current licenses in other states may apply by endorsement
                                                                                                       - - Requirements per OAC 435:10-4-7

                                                                                                                                                                                                   ## Lapsed License & Reinstatement Procedures
                                                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                   ### Three-Tier Reinstatement Framework
                                                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                   Oklahoma has three distinct categories for reinstatement based on lapse duration:
                                                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                   #### Category 1: Lapsed Less Than One Year
                                                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                   **Type of Process:** Automatic Renewal
                                                                                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                                   **Requirements:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                   - Must have completed necessary continuing education (CME) requirements
                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - CME verification required
                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - No background check required
                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - No re-examination required
                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - No new applicant status required
                                                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                           - **Renewal Process:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Login to Member Portal
                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Complete automatic renewal
                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Practice can resume immediately upon renewal completion
                                                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - **Timeline:** 1-2 weeks typical
                                                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - #### Category 2: Lapsed More Than One Year But Less Than Three Years
                                                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - **Type of Process:** Formal Reinstatement Application
                                                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - **Requirements:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Submit formal reinstatement application (not full new application)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Background check MAY be required (Board discretion)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - Re-examination MAY be required (Board discretion)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Does NOT require full reapplication as new applicant
                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - CME documentation required showing compliance during lapsed period
                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - **Application Process:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - File written application with Board
                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Describe actions taken to comply with Board requirements
                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Include all supporting documentation
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Secretary reviews for completeness
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - If compliant: scheduled for Board action
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - If noncompliant: applicant notified of deficiencies in writing
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Applicant may request Board review of Secretary determination (requires additional fee)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - **Board Discretion:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - The Board exercises discretion on background checks and re-examination based on:
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           -   - Reason for lapse
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               -   - Physician's history
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   -   - Time since lapse
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       -   - Compliance status
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - **Timeline:** 4-8 weeks typical
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - #### Category 3: Lapsed More Than Three Years
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - **Type of Process:** New Application (Full Reapplication)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - **Requirements:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Must start application process completely over
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Treated as new initial licensure applicant
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Background check IS REQUIRED
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Re-examination IS REQUIRED
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - All original licensure requirements must be met again
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - Full credentialing and verification packages required
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - Evidence of status documentation required for qualified aliens
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Medical degree verification required
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Postgraduate training verification required
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Pass required examinations per current standards
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - **Application Process:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Use MD Initial License Application portal
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Submit all documents required for initial licensure
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Background check will be conducted
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - Must pass examinations per current Board rules
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - All current licensure requirements apply
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - **Timeline:** 8-12 weeks typical (similar to initial licensure)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - ### Statutory Authority for Reinstatement
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - **OAC 435:5-1-6 - Reinstatement Procedures:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Licensee bears burden of demonstrating compliance with all Board-imposed terms and conditions
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Board may:
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             -   - Deny reinstatement if licensee fails to satisfy compliance requirements
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 -   - Approve reinstatement without restriction
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     -   - Approve reinstatement with probation or restrictions to protect public health/safety
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Applicant must submit written application setting forth:
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           -   - Actions taken to comply with Board conditions
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               -   - All supporting documentation of compliance
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Secretary reviews application
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - - If Secretary finds compliance with all Board requirements, matter scheduled for Board action
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       - - If Secretary finds noncompliance, applicant notified in writing and matter NOT scheduled for Board action
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Applicant may petition for Board review of Secretary determination (requires fee payment)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - ### Return to Practice Considerations
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - The Board provides a **Return to Practice Memorandum** for physicians applying for reinstatement after a leave of absence. This document addresses specific requirements for physicians returning to practice after extended periods. Reference this document when planning reinstatement after significant lapse.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - ### Opioid Prescribing Limits
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - #### Acute Pain Prescribing
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - **Supply Limits:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         - - Maximum 7-day supply of opioids for acute pain
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           - - Applies to most acute pain conditions
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             - - Example: For acute fracture or postoperative pain
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - **Exemptions:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               - - Cancer pain
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 - - Palliative/hospice care
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   - - Post-surgical pain as prescribed by surgeon
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - #### Chronic Pain Management
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - **Patient Evaluation Requirements (OAC 435:10-7-11):**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     - 1. Medical history and physical examination (including vital signs)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       2. 2. Assessment of pain's effect on physical and psychological function
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          3. 3. Documentation of patient's history of substance abuse
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             4. 4. Diagnostic, therapeutic, and laboratory results
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                5. 5. Documentation of all evaluations and consultations
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   6. 6. Follow-up evaluations
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      7. 7. Clear treatment objectives
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         8. **Mandatory Written Treatment Plan:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         9. - State objectives for determining treatment success (pain relief, improved function)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            - - Indicate if further diagnostic evaluations are planned
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              - - Adjust drug therapy to individual patient medical needs
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                - - Consider other treatment modalities or rehabilitation programs based on pain etiology
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  - **Patient-Provider Agreement Requirements:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  - - Discuss risks and benefits of controlled substances with patient
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - - Patient should receive prescriptions from ONE physician and ONE pharmacy only
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - - For high-risk patients OR patients with history of substance abuse: MANDATORY written patient-provider agreement covering:
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        -   - Urine/serum medication levels screening when requested
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            -   - Number and frequency of prescription refills
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                -   - Reasons for which drug therapy may be discontinued (e.g., violation of agreement)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - **Periodic Review Requirements:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - - Periodically review course of pain treatment
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - - Monitor new information about pain etiology and patient health status
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - - Continuation or modification depends on progress toward treatment objectives
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          - - Satisfactory response indicated by:
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            -   - Decreased pain
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                -   - Increased level of function
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    -   - Improved quality of life
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - - Monitor objective evidence of improved or diminished function
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          - - Consider input from family members/caregivers
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            - **Consultation & Referral:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            - - Willing to refer to specialists as necessary to achieve treatment objectives
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              - - Special attention to patients at risk for medication misuse, abuse, or diversion
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                - - Extra care, monitoring, documentation, and consultation required for patients with:
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  -   - History of substance abuse
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      -   - Comorbid psychiatric disorders
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          -   - High-risk medication profiles
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              - **Compliance with Laws & Regulations:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              - - Physicians must be licensed in Oklahoma
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                - - Must comply with all federal and state laws regarding controlled substances
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  - - Reference: Physicians Manual of the U.S. Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - - Reference applicable state regulations
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - ### Benzodiazepine Prescribing Restrictions
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - **General Restrictions:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - - Benzodiazepine prescribing restrictions exist but specific limits not fully detailed in available Board materials
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - - Check current state statutes (Title 63) for specific quantity and duration limits
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          - - Patient-provider agreements recommended for benzodiazepine therapy similar to opioid therapy
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            - **Caution with Opioid Combinations:**
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            - - Special care when prescribing benzodiazepines to patients also taking opioids
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              - - Increased overdose risk
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                - - Enhanced monitoring required

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  - - Oklahoma Prescription Monitoring Program is mandatory

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - - Check for "doctor shopping" and multiple fills
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        - **PMP Portal:** https://www.okmedicalboard.org/professional-resources (see Prescription Monitoring Program link)

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            - ### Telemedicine & Controlled Substance Prescribing
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            - **General Telemedicine Allowed:** Yes, with specific requirements
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            - **Opioid Prescribing via Telemedicine:** PROHIBITED
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            - - OAC 435:10-7-13(d)(3)(B)(ii) explicitly prohibits telemedicine for opioid prescribing
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              - - Telemedicine cannot be used to establish physician-patient relationship for prescribing:
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                -   - Opiates
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    -   - Synthetic opiates
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        -   - Semisynthetic opiates
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            -   - Benzodiazepines
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                -   - Carisoprodol
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    - **Exception:** Telemedicine may be used to prescribe:
