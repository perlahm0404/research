## SYSTEMATIC GAPS (Across All 68 Documents)

### 1. Missing v3.0 Frontmatter (100% of documents)
**Impact:** -17 points average per document

**Missing Sections:**
- `governance:` (framework, authority_level, statutes) - 97% missing
- `soc2_compliance:` (scope, data_classification, etc.) - 100% missing
- `iso_standards:` (applicable standards, approval_status) - 100% missing
- `scope:` (included/excluded arrays) - 95% missing
- `critical_gaps:`, `high_gaps:`, `medium_gaps:` arrays - 100% missing

**Remediation:** Apply standardized v3.0 frontmatter template to all documents (+15-18 points)

### 2. Missing Verbatim Quotes (95% of [FACT] tags)
**Impact:** -4 to -5 points per document

**Current State:**
- Citations present: 90-95% of [FACT] tags
- URLs present: 60-90% of [FACT] tags
- **Verbatim quotes: <5% of [FACT] tags**

**Example of Gap:**
```
CURRENT:
[FACT - STATUTE] Georgia requires 40 hours biennial.
Citation: O.C.G.A. § 43-34-11
URL: https://law.justia.com/codes/georgia/...

NEEDED (v3.0 standard):
[FACT - STATUTE] Georgia requires 40 hours biennial.
Citation: O.C.G.A. § 43-34-11
URL: https://law.justia.com/codes/georgia/...
Quote: "Each physician shall complete a minimum of forty (40) hours
of continuing medical education during each twenty-four (24) month period."
Last Verified: 2026-01-02
```

**Remediation:** Add verbatim regulatory quotes to all [FACT - STATUTE] and [FACT - ADMIN_CODE] tags (+4-5 points)

### 3. Incomplete Source Hierarchy (60% of documents)
**Impact:** -1 to -2 points per document

**Gap:** Source hierarchy for conflict resolution not explicitly documented as separate section

**Remediation:** Add SOURCE HIERARCHY subsection to all "Sources Cited" sections (+1-2 points)

## KEY INSIGHTS

### 1. v3.0 Standard Works
Documents claiming 85% completion (v3.0) are remarkably accurate (+/- 5%). The 85% threshold appears properly calibrated to v3.0 requirements.

### 2. Frontmatter = Systematic Failure Point
100% of documents fail complete frontmatter validation. This is NOT a research quality issue - it's a template implementation gap. Content quality is good; structure is missing.

### 3. Evidence Tagging = Major Success
Documents extensively use [FACT], [INFERENCE], [CRITICAL GAP] tags. The evidence classification system is adopted and working well.

### 4. Verbatim Quotes = Universal Gap
<5% of statutory [FACT] tags include verbatim quotes. This is the single easiest quick-win improvement (+4-5 points per document, 2 hours work).

### 5. Split-Board Excellence
All TIER-2 states have exemplary MD vs DO comparison tables. Florida's table is gold-standard for rules engine integration.
