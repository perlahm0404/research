            ## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

            Oklahoma's medical licensing renewal system for MDs is governed primarily by Title 59 of the Oklahoma Statutes (O.S. ยง 480-518.1) and Title 435 of the Oklahoma Administrative Code (OAC). Key findings include:

            - **CME Requirement:** 60 Category 1 hours within every 3-year renewal cycle
            - - **Grace Period:** 3 years from initial licensure or reinstatement before CME reporting begins
              - - **Renewal Cycle:** Triennial (3 years)
                - - **Online Renewal:** Available at https://pay.apps.ok.gov/medlic/md/
                  - - **Renewal Notice:** Mailed ~60 days before expiration
                    - - **Primary Authority:** Oklahoma State Board of Medical Licensure and Supervision (OBMLS)
                     
                      - **FSMB Discrepancies Identified:**
                      - - Grace period not mentioned in FSMB comparison documents
                        - - Specific CME hour requirements need verification against current FSMB data

                          ## LIFECYCLE PHASES

                          ### Phase 1: First Renewal Cycle

                          **Grace Period for Newly Licensed Physicians:**
                          - **Enabled:** YES
                          - - **Duration:** 3 years from date of initial licensure
                            - - **CME Requirement During Grace:** Not required to report CME until 3 years have passed from license issuance date
                              - - **Source:** Licensing FAQ - Oklahoma Medical Board
                                - - **Quote:** "Newly licensed physicians and physicians whose licenses have been reinstated will be required to begin reporting three years from the date of licensure/reinstatement."
                                 
                                  - **One-Time Requirements for First Renewal:**
                                  - - Information gathering in progress - checking for HIV/AIDS, child abuse, human trafficking requirements
                                   
                                    - **Documentation for First Renewal:**
                                    - - Must certify CME completion if past grace period
                                      - - Standard renewal attestation requirements
                                       
                                        - ### Phase 2: Ongoing Renewals (After First Cycle)
                                       
                                        - **Renewal Period:** 3 years (Triennial)
                                       
                                        - **CME Requirements - Steady State:**
                                        - - **Total Hours Required:** 60 Category 1 CME hours
                                          - - **Reporting Period:** Within the preceding 3 years
                                            - - **Frequency:** Every 3-year renewal cycle
                                              - - **Source:** Licensing FAQ, Oklahoma Medical Board
