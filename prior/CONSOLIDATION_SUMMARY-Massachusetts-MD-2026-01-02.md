## KEY FINDINGS NOW DOCUMENTED

### CME Requirements (FULLY DOCUMENTED)

**Total Hours:**
- Full License: 50 credits per 2-year cycle
- Administrative License: 50 credits per 2-year cycle
- Temporary Faculty: 25 credits per 2-year cycle

**Complete Topic List (9 Requirements):**
1. ✅ Cognitive Impairments (Alzheimer's/Dementia) - 1 credit, ONE-TIME
2. ✅ Child Abuse Reporting Training - ONE-TIME
3. ✅ Domestic Violence & Sexual Violence Training - ONE-TIME
4. ✅ EHR Proficiency - 3 credits, ONE-TIME (if practicing)
5. ✅ End-of-Life Care - 2 credits, ONE-TIME
6. ✅ Opioid Education & Pain Management - 3 credits, CONDITIONAL (if prescribing)
7. ✅ Risk Management Study - 10 credits (Full) / 5 credits (Temp Faculty), RECURRING
8. ✅ Implicit Bias Training - 2 credits, ONE-TIME (applications after 6/1/2022)
9. ✅ Reading Board Regulations (243 CMR 1.00, 2.00 & 3.00) - 2 credits, RECURRING

**Lifecycle Phase Status:**
- ✅ First Renewal Requirements: Documented
- ✅ Ongoing Renewal Requirements: Documented
- ✅ Lapsed License Revival Requirements: Documented

### Renewal Process (FULLY DOCUMENTED)

- ✅ Renewal frequency: Biennial (every 2 years on birthday)
- ✅ Renewal fee: $600
- ✅ Online portal: Required
- ✅ Renewal deadline: Physician's birthday
- ✅ Payment processor: nCourt (third-party)
- ✅ Attestation requirement: CME completion attestation in portal
- ✅ License status options: Active, Inactive, Retired

### Verification & Audit (SUBSTANTIALLY DOCUMENTED)

- ✅ Verification method: Online portal attestation (no certificate upload required)
- ✅ Audit type: FOR-CAUSE ONLY (not routine random audits)
- ✅ Audit triggers: Investigations of substandard care or dishonesty
- ✅ Current audit status: Board NOT currently conducting routine CME audits

### Statutory Authority (DOCUMENTED)

- ✅ Primary statute: M.G.L. c. 112, § 2
- ✅ Administrative code: 243 CMR 1.00, 2.00, 3.00
- ✅ Related policies: Policy 17-05, 19-06, 21-04, 94-05, 15-05, 03-03
