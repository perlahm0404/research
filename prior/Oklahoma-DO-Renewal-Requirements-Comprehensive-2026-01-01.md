**Research Date:** January 1, 2026

## Executive Summary

Oklahoma DO physicians have significantly different requirements from MD physicians, most notably operating on an **annual** renewal cycle with 16 AOA Category 1 CME hours required per year. This represents a substantial departure from the MD triennial system. DOs can substitute AMA Category 1 hours if they hold ABMS board certification. The state requires a biennial 3-hour CME course (specific topic TBD), and renewal must occur before July 1st each year. An annual renewal fee of $250.00 applies.

---

## License Renewal Requirements

### Renewal Frequency & Deadline

**[FACT]** Oklahoma DO physicians must renew their licenses annually before July 1st.

**Source:** Multiple CME provider sites and https://oklahoma.gov/osboe/faqsoklahoma.gov  
**Quote:** "All licenses must be renewed each year prior to July 1st"

**[CRITICAL DIFFERENCE FROM MD]** Unlike MD physicians who operate on a 3-year CME tracking cycle, DO physicians renew annually with annual CME hour requirements.

### Total CME Hours Required

**[FACT]** Oklahoma DO physicians must complete 16 AOA Category 1 CME hours annually.
